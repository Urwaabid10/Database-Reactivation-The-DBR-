## Overview

Med-Spas often accumulate large databases of inactive contacts who have not engaged or purchased services for extended periods. These dormant leads represent a significant missed revenue opportunity.

This project implements a high-intensity, reply-based automation system designed to re-engage inactive contacts, generate immediate conversations, and convert them into high-intent leads without relying on paid advertising.

---

## Problem Statement

* Large database of inactive contacts (no purchases for over a year)
* Missed opportunities to generate revenue from existing leads
* Heavy reliance on paid ads for new customer acquisition
* No structured reactivation or follow-up system

---

## Solution

An automated reactivation workflow that:

* Targets inactive contacts using tag-based triggers
* Initiates personalized, conversational SMS outreach
* Encourages quick responses through simple questions
* Identifies high-intent leads based on replies
* Routes engaged contacts into a high-priority pipeline

---

## Key Features

### Tag-Based Workflow Trigger

* Workflow is initiated when a specific tag is applied (e.g., "Reactivation-April")
* Enables bulk targeting of inactive contacts efficiently

---

### Personalized SMS Outreach

* Sends a simple, human-like message such as:

  * "Are you still interested in our services?"
* Designed to maximize reply rates and engagement

---

### Reply-Based Automation Logic

* Uses "Wait for Response" functionality
* Monitors incoming replies and determines engagement

---

### Positive Intent Detection and Routing

* When a lead responds with interest:

  * Internal notification is sent to the business owner
  * Contact is moved to a "Hot Lead" stage in the pipeline

---

### Pipeline Integration

* Reactivated leads are automatically organized for follow-up
* Ensures high-intent contacts are prioritized

---

## Workflow Summary

1. Contacts are tagged for reactivation (e.g., "Reactivation-April")
2. Workflow is triggered automatically
3. System sends a personalized SMS message
4. Waits for the contact's response
5. If positive intent is detected:

   * Notify the owner
   * Move contact to "Hot Lead" pipeline
6. Sales team follows up with re-engaged leads
